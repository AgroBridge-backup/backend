## English

### 1. Core Business Model Assumptions

Our revenue model is a SaaS subscription tiered by producer size and platform usage.

*   **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** Estimated at **$500** per producer.
*   **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU):** **$150/month** averaged across tiers.
*   **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV):** Estimated at **$5,400** (36-month lifetime).
*   **Gross Margin:** Approximately **85%**.

### 2. "What-If" Scenario Calculator

This simplified calculator demonstrates how investment can scale revenue. *This is an illustrative model. A detailed financial model is available under NDA.*

|:----------------:|:-----------------------------:|:----------------------------------:|:------------------------------------:|
| **$100,000**     | 200                           | $360,000                           | 260%                                 |
| **$250,000**     | 500                           | $900,000                           | 260%                                 |
| **$500,000**     | 1,000                         | $1,800,000                         | 260%                                 |

### 3. Transformational Value Proposition

Our value is not just in software, but in transformation. The "before and after" for our clients is our strongest metric.

> **Before AgroBridge:** High uncertainty, manual paper processes, limited market access, ~15-20% product rejection rates from international buyers due to lack of trust.
>
> **After AgroBridge:** Verifiable trust via the blockchain, automated data collection, access to premium markets, **product rejection rates drop to <5%**. This delta represents a direct and massive ROI for our customers.
